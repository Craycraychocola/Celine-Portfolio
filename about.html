Tell Me More!

Hello! I am a 15 year old female, born on 30th of March, 2004. I am half Malaysian-Chinese and half Italian. I am currently doing homeschooling, and working as a part-time assistant to CassVinn Acupuncture and Beauty Center.

I have experienced many adventures that has shaped my path to what it is today. I would call myself lucky as many other would not have these kinds of opportunity, so I will always overcome what life throws at me, to become stronger.

From a very young age, I stayed on a boat nearly every summer and winter holidays, sailing to different parts of Thailand, discovering my love for snorkeling and marine animals. At age 14, I lived by myself in Taiwan for a year to develop my Mandarin and maturity.

However, as I started studing computer programming at Kidocode, everything changed for me! Thanks to them, I now know how to code with many different languages, and understand the complexity of how to make proper websites and games. I'm undeniably thankful for being able to study and develop such amazing friendship here.
