3D Printing

Where your imaginations can be brought to life.

3D printing first came out in 1983 by Chuck Hall, originally known as Rapid Prototyping. It has developed and evolved to the point that doctors are printing organs for patients, and astronauts are printing food in space! It truly is unbelieveable how far 3D printing has reached, and unimaginable how much more it can develop.

I chose to learn 3D printing as I was fasinated with the machine's ability to produce a complex and detail model. Although I've only scratched the surface with 3D printing, I just can't wait for more to come.
